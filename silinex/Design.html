Logo Clear Space & Rules
  • Minimum clear space = logo height × 0.5 on all sides

  • Approved colors: Deep Navy, Corporate Blue, White

Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

Silinex communicates as a confident, knowledgeable technology partner — authoritative but accessible, aspirational but grounded.

✅ Do

  • Use confident, action-oriented language ("deliver", "empower", "drive")
  • Emphasize outcomes and business value
  • Keep sentences clear and concise
  • Use "we" and "our" to express partnership
  • Highlight expertise with proof ("100+ years combined experience")
  • Use industry terminology appropriately (GRC, AMS, ERP)
  • Convey trust through specificity and facts

❌ Don't

  • Use vague, passive language ("solutions may help you...")
  • Overuse buzzwords without substance
  • Use casual or overly informal tone
  • Write overly long, jargon-heavy sentences
  • Undermine confidence with qualifiers ("we try to...")
  • Use generic claims without data points
  • Sound transactional instead of partner-oriented

Iconography

Icon & Imagery Style

Silinex uses flat, outlined SVG icons in primary brand colors, paired with professional photography and custom illustrations.

Icon Characteristics
  • ✦ Outlined SVG format, 24×24px base
  • ✦ Stroke weight: 1.5–2px
  • ✦ Colors: Corporate Blue, Orange, Teal, White
  • ✦ Placed on tinted backgrounds (blue-light, orange-light)
  • ✦ Rounded corners on container icons — 10px radius
Photography / Imagery
  • ✦ Professional, diverse team photography
  • ✦ Technology-forward: screens, servers, data
  • ✦ Clean, bright, corporate environments
  • ✦ Slightly cool color grade to match palette
  • ✦ Hero imagery: people + technology side by side
